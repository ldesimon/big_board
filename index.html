Why we're here

Learning to build
together.

The barrier to creating functional tools has never been lower. A prototype that once required a developer and a long timeline can now be built by anyone with a good idea. We want to capture that momentum and figure out how to best support it.

💡
Making work visible

By putting our ideas in one place, we can find collaborators, avoid duplicating efforts, and celebrate the creative solutions people are designing for their own workflows.

🌱
Defining the support

We are using this board to learn. Seeing what gets built helps us understand what kind of hosting, data access, and engineering support our teams actually need to scale their ideas.

The framework

What makes a tool.

To help guide our learning, we're asking a few simple questions about each project. This helps us start a dialogue about the building blocks required to share or scale an idea safely and effectively.

Block 01
Current Stage

Is it a chat prompt, a rough prototype, or actively being used? Knowing the stage helps calibrate expectations.

Target Audience

Who is this for? A personal workflow tool requires a very different setup than an org-wide application.
